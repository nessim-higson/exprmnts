On the "physics" question

Decompiling the ActionScript in each binary: the nav is text-based and motion-driven, but what reads as "physics" is eased tweening, not a spring/mass simulation. The 2003–2007 builds (AS2) use buildMainNav / textNavAni with ease-in curves; the 2010 fb7 build (AS3) drives an elastic/bounce tween engine — springy feel, scripted keyframes underneath.

No velocity, gravity, friction or drag-constraint code is present in these homepage binaries. If the physics nav you remember was a separate lab/experiment piece, say the word and I'll go dig that specific one out of the archive.
